Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 20, 2026

Kalyx is a puzzle game developed by Dawntime Studio. This policy explains what information is and isn't collected when you use the app.

No account required

Kalyx does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to play.

Data stored on your device

Your puzzle progress, statistics, and streaks are stored locally on your device only. This information is never transmitted to us or any server — we don't operate one.

Advertising

Kalyx displays ads served by Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google's Mobile Ads SDK may collect device identifiers (such as your advertising ID) and other information as described in Google's Privacy Policy. We do not control what Google collects for this purpose beyond configuring the ad units themselves.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or a U.S. state with applicable privacy law, you will be shown a consent choice on first launch allowing you to control whether ads are personalized.

No data sale

We do not sell your data. We don't collect enough data to have anything to sell — the game has no backend, no analytics beyond what the ad SDK itself performs, and no account system.

Children's privacy

Kalyx is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the update will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to dawntimestudio.bc [at] gmail.com.
